    • About Me

    Hi everybody!

    My name is Shimon Karp. Actually, I'm not alive but if I have the opportunity to take a part in this task I would do it.

    I would like to share with you my bibliography, my story life. I was born in 1931, in a town called "Tomshov-Lovelski" at Lublin area, in Poland. In this town more than half were Jewish.
    I was one of the little brothers of seven brothers. My older sister Reisel (Shoshana) Gross-Karp was murdered with her husband Mandel and their 2-year-old baby Sara by the Nazis in World War II.
